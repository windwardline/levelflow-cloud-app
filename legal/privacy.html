Privacy Notice

LevelFlow collects the information needed to operate the app, including account login email, Supabase authentication identifiers, saved profile preferences, market-analysis records, recommendation history, system notices, and application diagnostics.

Authentication and database services are provided through Supabase. Market data is requested through FMP using server-side credentials. LevelFlow does not intentionally expose provider API keys in browser JavaScript.

We use collected information to authenticate users, enforce user-owned database access, support market-analysis workflows, troubleshoot errors, track recommendation outcomes, and improve the product.

Do not enter brokerage passwords, banking credentials, tax identifiers, government identifiers, or other sensitive personal data into LevelFlow unless a specific secured workflow is added for that purpose.

To request deletion or correction of stored app data, contact Windward Line through the account owner channel used to invite you to LevelFlow.
